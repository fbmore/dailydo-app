Getting started

Tap the + button and just type — DailyDo figures out whether you're writing a task, a note, a link, or a timer, and files it accordingly. You can always change the type before saving.

Capturing from other apps

Use the system share sheet from any app (Safari, Photos, Mail…) and choose DailyDo. Links arrive with their titles, images and files arrive as documents, and text is analyzed the same way as in-app capture.

Reminders aren't firing

  • Check that notifications are allowed: Settings → Notifications → DailyDo.
  • Make sure Focus modes aren't silencing the app.
  • Tasks need a due time (not just a date) to trigger an alert.

My items aren't syncing across devices

DailyDo syncs through your private iCloud. Make sure all devices are signed in to the same Apple Account with iCloud Drive enabled (Settings → [your name] → iCloud). Sync happens automatically in the background, usually within seconds; large attachments can take longer on slow connections.

Scanning, photos, and voice notes

The first time you scan a document, take a photo, or record a voice note, iOS asks for camera or microphone permission. If you declined and want to enable it later: Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera / Microphone → DailyDo. Voice recordings are transcribed automatically; transcription quality depends on your device's language settings.

Privacy

Your data stays on your devices and in your own private iCloud — there's no account and no analytics. See the Privacy Policy for details.
